My name is Darshni Banwarie, I'm 23 years old and I study Communication and Multimedia Design at the Hogeschool van Amsterdam. I'm currently in my 4th year. I was born and raised in Paramaribo, Suriname. I moved to the Netherlands when I was 18 to pursue further education.

During my education I mainly focused on User Experience Design. I enjoy working in creative teams because that's when we can come up with the best solutions for design problems. I think it is important to be well prepared, to work neatly and structured. I am a hard worker with an eye for detail. Because of my sense of responsibility and the ability to plan tasks well, I ensure that I always finish projects on time.

In my culture-rich motherland I learned several languages such as Dutch, English, Sarnami, Sranantongo and un poquito Spanish. My knowledge of various backgrounds make it possible for me to put myself in users' shoes and solving issues that at first seem impossible.

I love connecting with people and learning from them. Besides that I work really well in teams, because I'm a teamplayer, probably because I'm most creative when I work with other equally creative people. And I can also handle feedback and adjust my work well. I'm a very open person for discussions.

I learned in my second year of education that I absolutely love User Experience Design, so I took every opportunity to learn more about it by partaking in UX projects. Because I think it's interesting to try and understand people their perspectives, their frustrations, their workflows and their joys. It's fun to see how the solution can improve people's lives and how much easier they can accomplish something.
