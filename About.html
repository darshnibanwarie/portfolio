My name is Darshni Banwarie, I'm 23 years old and I study Communication and Multimedia Design at the Hogeschool van Amsterdam. I'm currently in my 4th year. I was born and raised in Paramaribo, Suriname. I moved to the Netherlands when I was 18 to pursue further education.

In my culture-rich motherland I learned several languages such as Dutch, English, Sarnami, Sranantongo and un poquito Spanish.

I learned in my second year of education that I absolutely love User Experience Design, so I took every opportunity to learn more about it by partaking in UX projects.

I love connecting with people and learning. I work really well in teams because I'm a teamplayer. I think I'm most creative when I work with other equally creative people. And I can also handle feedback and adjust my work well. I'm a very open person for discussions.

UI & Visual Design

I'm a skilled visual designer with an eye for typography, layout, graphics and color. I love building functional software & mobile products and implementing modern aesthetics.

User Research

I believe that user research is the most important step of the design process. I love speaking to users and understanding their process & problems first, before even considering design.

Prototyping

I'm skilled in prototyping products with users and stakeholders across the entire design lifecycle. I love using the Figma tool to wireframe and prototype designs.

Cognitive Psychology

I've taken a course on consumer psychology and understanding how users think, behave, and interact with products. I believe in analyzing user behavior for an interaction science-backed approach.
