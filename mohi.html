Mohi app

This was my favorite project of the minor User Experience Design. I focused on the information architecture of the existing Mohi app for 6 weeks. This project was on behalf of the founder of the Mohi app, Wibe van de Vijfer.

The Mohi community wants to introduce a slow media platform where they want to share content with the members in a different way. They want to share media that is more personal. You can think of your favorite podcasts, documentaries and articles.

Process

In the first weeks I conducted research using user tests among existing users and new users to map their frustrations, needs and wants. With this information I was able to improve the existing app.

It turned out that the users couldn't find the most important functionalities (add content, add highlights, settings, view my profile) and wanted to find them easier. After this I did research on how I could adapt the information architecture to the wishes of the users.

In the project I used the following research methods:

  • Product requirement list
  • Deskresearch
  • Ideation
  • Research on sitemaps
  • User tests
  • Peer design critique
